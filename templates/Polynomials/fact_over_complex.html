Example

Question: Find factors over complex numbers of x³+3x²+9x+27.

Solution:

x³ + 3x² + 9x +27 =(x³)+(3x²)+(-3ix²)+(-9ix)+(3ix²)+(9ix)+(9x)+(27)

=((x²)(x)+(3x)(x)+(-3ix)(x)+(-9i)(x)+(x²)(3i)+(3x)(3i)+(-3ix)(3i)+(-9i)(3i))

=((x²)((x + 3i))+(3x)((x + 3i))+(-3ix)((x + 3i))+(-9i)((x + 3i)))

=((x)(x)+(3)(x)+(x)(-3i)+(3)(-3i))(x + 3i)

=((x)((x - 3i))+(3)((x - 3i)))(x + 3i)

=(x+3)(x-3i)(x+3i)
